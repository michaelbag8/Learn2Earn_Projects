**Prompt:** List four types of noun

**AI Model**
# Four Types of Nouns
1. **Common Noun** – Refers to a general name of a person, place, or thing.  
   *Examples:* teacher, city, dog  

2. **Proper Noun** – Refers to a specific person, place, or organization and always begins with a capital letter.   

3. **Concrete Noun** – Refers to something you can see, touch, hear, smell, or taste.  
   *Examples:* table, music, flower  

4. **Abstract Noun** – Refers to ideas, qualities, or states that cannot be seen or touched.  
   *Examples:* love, freedom, honesty

# Benefits of Sleep for Adults

## 1. Improves Brain Function
- Enhances memory and learning  
- Increases focus and productivity  
- Supports problem-solving and decision-making  

## 2. Strengthens the Immune System
- Helps the body fight infections  
- Reduces the risk of chronic illnesses  

## 3. Supports Heart Health
- Lowers blood pressure  
- Reduces risk of heart disease and stroke  

## 4. Regulates Mood and Mental Health
- Reduces stress and anxiety  
- Lowers risk of depression  
- Improves emotional stability  

## 5. Aids Physical Recovery
- Repairs muscles and tissues  
- Supports hormone balance  
- Boosts energy levels  

## 6. Helps Maintain Healthy Weight
- Regulates hunger hormones  
- Reduces cravings and overeating  

**Recommended sleep:** 7–9 hours per night for adults.
