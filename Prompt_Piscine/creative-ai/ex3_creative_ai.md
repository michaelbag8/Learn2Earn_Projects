## Refine the prompt by adding constraints such as art style, colors, or perspective.

## **Prompt:**

Create an image of an African village girl standing on a riverbank, looking nervous before jumping into the water below. It’s daytime, with warm sunlight filtering through lush green trees. Capture her hesitation through her posture and facial expression.
Realistic with soft painterly touches similar to contemporary African portrait art.
the color palette should greens, warm yellows, and soft blues.Slightly low angle from the river’s edge, focusing on the girl’s face and body language.

## Comparision of the prompts output

Prompt 1 is very open ended. It gives the subject, location, and basic emotion, but it doesn’t guide the model on how the image should look. There’s no art style, no color direction, no perspective.while prompt 2 was more descriptive and detailed, it has art style, color and perpective.
